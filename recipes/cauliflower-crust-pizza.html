Cauliflower Crust Pizza

Description

This is the recipe for Stephanie's cauliflower crust pizza.

Ingredients

  • cauliflower crust
  • pizza sauce
  • shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • pepperoni
  • marinated grilled artichoke hearts
  • kalamata olives
  • oregano
  • red pepper flakes
  • garlic powder
  • chili oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to the temperature specified on the packaging for your crust.
  2. While the oven heats, prepare the pizza toppings by counting out the desired amount of pepperoni, olives, and artichokes. Halve the olives and quarter the artichokes.
  3. Assemble pizza. Spread the sauce around the crust, evenly distribute the cheese, and decorate with toppings as desired.
  4. Bake the pizza. The crust packaging should indicate a range of time (e.g., 12-14 minutes). Set your timer for the lower time and check on the pizza when your alarm goes off. Cook for an extra couple of minutes if need be. You want a nice bit of char on the edge of the artichokes and some dark spots on the cheese.
  5. Cut the pizza into quarters.
  6. Sprinkle with desired seasonings and enjoy!
